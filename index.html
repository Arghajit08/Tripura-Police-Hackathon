STATEMENT One

Beat Policing Diary(BPD APP) with integration of wanted criminals data.

Beat Policing is a significant day to day activity which is the rooted base of Police Department. The reports need to be analysed and linked with criminal activity as a proactive measure to combat any offensive activity in a locality. The conventional process of Beat policing has no mechanism to make a background linkage of trail of activity across police station. needs one database having list of wanted criminals with detail particulars so that if any person is arrested in any Police Station, it can be checked into the system instantly whether that person is wanted in any other case throughout the country.

STATEMENT Three

Integration of CCTV feeds and intelligent Search of CCTV contents

In case of crime occurs in any area, CCTV is a significant supplement and majour scorce of breakthrough in investigation. Therefore, police should know exactly where CCTV cameras are installed nearby of that Place of Occurrence of Crime. A geo-tagged location mapping to be there for any CCTV(Private and public) installation. This information to be easily searched on visual mapping layered with Google Map.

STATEMENT Four

Geospatial tagging of the accident areas.

Police Department needs to know the Geospatial Locations where frequent accidents are being occurred across the state. Regular input of lat long, type of accident, case number, casualty etc will be fed into the system. There will be search criteria in ascending/descending order to know the most accident prone area of a particular road etc so that those points may be inspected by a join team of Police and PWD and necessary repair of that portion of the road if required may be taken into consideration. In addition, warning signboard etc may be put for alertness of the drivers.

STATEMENT Five

Rapid identification of Vehicle Number plate under varying lighting condition.

Identification of vehicle number plate is a major concern of traffic control with law and order maintaining scenario for police department. The task is challenging due to varying lighting condition, speed of the vehicle and in some cases nonstandard numberplates. The task is even more challenging with varying size and shape of a vehicles. In many cases human involvement for continuous surveillance is very difficult therefore an automated number plate identification system is suggested, where a cost effective system can be deployed on highway or crowded road condition.
